PRESTONHQ — CYBRANCEE UPLOAD CHECKLIST
======================================

Your site root (httpdocs) MUST contain ALL of these paths:

  httpdocs/index.html
  httpdocs/.htaccess
  httpdocs/dashboard/index.html
  httpdocs/assets/css/app.css
  httpdocs/assets/js/config.js
  httpdocs/assets/js/api.js
  httpdocs/assets/js/dashboard.js

VERIFY IN BROWSER (all must show 200, NOT 404):
  https://prestonhq.com/assets/js/config.js
  https://prestonhq.com/assets/js/api.js
  https://prestonhq.com/assets/js/dashboard.js
  https://prestonhq.com/assets/css/app.css

COMMON MISTAKE:
  Uploading only dashboard/ without the assets/ folder.
  Putting assets inside dashboard/assets/ (WRONG).

CORRECT structure on server:
  httpdocs/
    assets/          <-- at ROOT, same level as dashboard/
    dashboard/
    index.html

After upload: Ctrl+Shift+R hard refresh on dashboard page.
